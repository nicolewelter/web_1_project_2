Grid Infographic

This is an example of the graphic design work that I have produced in the last three months. This is an infographic designed to inform the viewer to look a little closer at the design and see how the many key elements work together to create chaotic harmony all within the structure of a grid. Each title is designed to act as both the definition and the label based only on placement and nearby elements. Although it is not a basic infographic, it is important to remember that stepping outside the box with catch the attention of many, whether it is good or bad.
